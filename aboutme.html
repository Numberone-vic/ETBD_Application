Where I Come From

I grew up in Cleveland, Ohio where I learned the importance of resilience and determination. Growing up in the city taught me how to stay focused on my goals and work hard even when things were difficult.

Throughout school I developed strong interests in both athletics and technology. Wrestling helped build my discipline, while technology sparked my curiosity about how systems work and how problems can be solved with innovation.
